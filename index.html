Our Signature Smoothies

Berry Blast

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ½ cup almond milk

Steps:

  1. Add almond milk to blender first
  2. Blend berries and banana for 30 seconds
  3. Add yogurt and honey, blend 1 minute
  4. Serve immediately!

Green Detox

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach
  • ½ cup pineapple
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup coconut water

Steps:

  1. Blend spinach and coconut water first
  2. Add pineapple and banana, blend 1 minute
  3. Top with chia seeds

Tropical Sunrise

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mango
  • ½ cup orange juice
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup coconut water
  • 1 tsp ginger

Steps:

  1. Blend mango and orange juice first
  2. Add banana and coconut water, blend 1 minute
  3. Garnish with ginger

Peanut Butter Banana

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp flaxseeds
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Blend bananas and almond milk first
  2. Add peanut butter, blend 1 minute
  3. Sprinkle flaxseeds and cinnamon

Chocolate Avocado

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 cup oat milk
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup

Steps:

  1. Blend avocado and banana first
  2. Add cocoa and oat milk, blend 1 minute
  3. Sweeten with maple syrup

Smoothie Pro Tips

  • Freeze fruits for thicker texture
  • Add greens first for smoother blending
  • Use ripe bananas for natural sweetness
